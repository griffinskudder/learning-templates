Urbanisation in the USA

Year 8 Geography — Urbanisation

What is Urbanisation?

Urbanisation means more people move to live in cities. Cities grow bigger when this happens. Today, more than half of all people in the world live in a city. The USA is one of the most urbanised countries in the world. About 83 out of every 100 Americans live in a city. 🐛

Why Do People Move to Cities?

People move to cities to find work. Cities have more jobs than the countryside. Cities also have schools, hospitals, and shops. In the USA, many people moved to cities when factories were built. Factories needed lots of workers. The workers needed places to live. 🐛 More people kept coming, so cities kept growing.

What Problems Do Cities Have?

When cities grow fast, problems can happen. There can be too many cars on the roads. This causes traffic jams and air pollution. Housing can be hard to find and very expensive. Some people in cities are very poor. Cities need good planning to look after everyone. 🐛
