Setup Guide (delete this box before sharing)
  1. Change the <h1> to your quiz title and the subtitle to your topic.
  2. Edit the five .sentence-card blocks below with your own sentences.
  3. Inside each sentence, find the <input class="blank"> element and set data-answer="correctWord" to the expected answer (case-insensitive).
  4. To add more questions, copy and paste a .sentence-card block and update the question number, sentence, and data-answer.
  5. Adjust colours and fonts in the :root block at the top of the <style> section.
