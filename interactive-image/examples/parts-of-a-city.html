Parts of a City

City Centre (CBD)

The CBD is the busy centre of the city.

It has tall buildings, shops, offices, and transport hubs.

Most people come here to work during the day.

Suburbs

Suburbs are areas around the edge of the city.

People live here in houses and flats.

They travel to the city centre for work or school.

Factories

Factories make goods and give people jobs.

They can cause air and water pollution near where they are built.

Many cities try to keep factories away from homes.

Parks

Parks are green spaces in the city.

They help clean the air and cool the city down.

They give people places to rest, play, and relax.

Transport

Transport includes roads, trains, and buses.

Good transport helps people move around the city.

Too many cars can cause traffic jams and pollution.
