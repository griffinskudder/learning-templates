How to customise this template
  1. Find the <ul id="timeline-list"> section below and edit the 6 <li> cards.
  2. On each card, set data-order="N" to indicate its correct position (1 = first, 6 = last).
  3. Change the card-title to your event name and card-description to a short detail or date.
  4. Update the page <title>, the <h1> heading, and the subtitle paragraph above.
  5. Optionally tweak colours in the :root CUSTOMIZATION ZONE in the <style> block.
  6. Delete this yellow instructions box when you are done.
