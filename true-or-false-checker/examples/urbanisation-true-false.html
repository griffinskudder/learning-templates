True or False?

Geography — Urbanisation — click True or False for each statement

Statement 1

More than half of all people in the world now live in a city.

True — for the first time in history, more people live in cities than in the countryside.

Statement 2

Australia is one of the most urbanised countries in the world.

True — about 9 out of every 10 Australians live in a city or large town.

Statement 3

Urbanisation means people move from cities to the countryside.

False — urbanisation means people move FROM the countryside TO cities. Cities get bigger.

Statement 4

Cities can cause pollution because of cars and factories.

True — cars and factories in cities produce gases that pollute the air.

Statement 5

Australia's cities are spread evenly across the whole country.

False — most Australian cities are on the coast. The middle of the country has very few people.
