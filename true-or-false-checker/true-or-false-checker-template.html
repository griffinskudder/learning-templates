Setup Guide (delete this section when done)

  1. Change the <h1> title and subtitle below to match your topic.
  2. Each statement is a <div class="statement-card"> block.
  3. Set data-answer="true" or data-answer="false" on each card to mark the correct answer.
  4. Edit the <p class="statement-text"> to write your statement.
  5. Edit the <p class="explanation"> to add an optional note shown after answering (or delete it).
  6. Copy the example block at the bottom of the list to add more statements.
  7. Customise colours by editing the CSS variables in the :root block at the top of <style>.

True or False?

General Knowledge — click True or False for each statement

Statement 1

The Great Wall of China is visible from space with the naked eye.

This is a common myth — the wall is far too narrow to see from space without optical aid.

Statement 2

Water boils at 100°C at sea level.

True — at standard atmospheric pressure, water boils at exactly 100°C (212°F). At higher altitudes, it boils at a lower temperature.

Statement 3

Sharks are fish, not mammals.

True — sharks are cartilaginous fish. Dolphins and whales look similar but are mammals; sharks are not.

Statement 4

Humans only use 10% of their brain.

False — brain scans show we use virtually all of our brain, and most areas are active at some point during the day.

Statement 5

Lightning can strike the same place twice.

True — tall structures like lightning rods and skyscrapers are struck repeatedly during the same storm.
