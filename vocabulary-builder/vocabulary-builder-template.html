How to set up your Vocabulary Builder

  1. Change the <title> tag and the <h1> heading to match your subject/topic.
  2. Edit the subtitle paragraph to describe your word set.
  3. For each <div class="card"> below, change:
    • The text inside <span class="word"> to your vocabulary word.
    • The text inside <span class="definition"> to the correct definition.
  4. To add more cards, copy the blank card template from the HTML comments and paste it inside <div class="card-grid">.
  5. Adjust colours by editing the CSS variables in the :root block at the top of the <style> section.
  6. Delete this yellow instructions banner when you are done.
